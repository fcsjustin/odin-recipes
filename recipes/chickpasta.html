Chicken Pasta

Description:

Chicken pasta is a delicious and versatile dish that combines tender pieces of chicken with flavorful pasta and a creamy or tomato-based sauce. Typically, the chicken is seasoned and cooked until golden brown, then tossed with your choice of pasta, such as penne, fettuccine, or spaghetti. The sauce can range from a rich Alfredo to a zesty marinara, providing a perfect complement to the chicken and pasta.

The dish is often finished with a sprinkle of fresh herbs like basil or parsley, and a generous grating of Parmesan cheese. It's a comforting meal that balances protein, carbs, and savory flavors, making it an ideal choice for a satisfying dinner. Whether served with a side of garlic bread or a crisp salad, chicken pasta is both hearty and delicious, perfect for any occasion.

  • 1 lb (450 g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
